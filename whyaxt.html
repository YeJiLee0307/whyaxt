Experience in Action

Brand Experience is where visual identity, content, space, and community meet. I am interested in how a brand can be experienced beyond a single image — through campaigns, offline moments, digital touchpoints, and the emotions people remember after encountering it.

Through Sogang Ribbon and Student Council projects, I have practiced planning and operating brand/community experiences in real contexts. These experiences helped me understand how ideas become touchpoints, how touchpoints build participation, and how participation turns into a stronger brand memory.
