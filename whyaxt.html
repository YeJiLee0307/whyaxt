Why Art & Technology?

I wanted to cherish both the sensitivity with which I perceive the world and the skills to bring those perceptions to life.

I didn’t want to stop at imagining — I wanted to create, to express, and to build.

Art & Technology breaks down the boundary between imagination and realization,
allowing me to better understand myself and to grow beyond my limits.

Through this journey, I aspire to create a world where creativity and logic, emotion and technology exist in harmony.
Art & Technology, to me, represents pure possibility.
